about me

I see myself as a Web Developer with a natural knack for combining technical know-how with creative elements. Well-versed in HTML, CSS, CMS, and a solid understanding JavaScript, Node, Reactjs, Mongodb, MySql, and ORMs. A lifelong learner and firm believer that education should never stop. Always expanding my knowledge and experience by keeping abreast of evolving trends, techniques, and technologies to streamline efficiency.

Why Code?

Building websites and applications is it’s own reward! I get to touch the lives of the people using the product and know they’re having a better digital experience because of the products we build. A person filling out forms in a Doctor's Office, taking an SAT or looking for the best College; they are focused on task at hand and not how to navigate the application and I get to be part of the team building that experience. Meeting people from so many different professions has given me a skill set that can’t be tough at any University, it’s my favorite part of being a Developer. Working with restaurant owners, be it to redesign their website, or create a new site, is my absolute favorite assignment!
